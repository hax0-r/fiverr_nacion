Costa Rica Volcanoes

Costa Rica is home to over 200 volcanic formations, with 7 currently active volcanoes. Of these, 5 are accessible to tourists, offering incredible opportunities to explore dramatic landscapes, hike to volcanic craters, and relax in natural hot springs. Discover the power and beauty of Costa Rica’s volcanoes, from the iconic Arenal to the breathtaking Poás, and experience one of the most unique natural wonders in the world.

Top 10 Must-Visit Destinations in Costa Rica

  • Arenal Volcano: Hike to the base, relax in hot springs, and enjoy stunning views.
  • Monteverde Cloud Forest: Walk across suspension bridges and spot exotic wildlife.
  • Manuel Antonio National Park: Explore lush rainforests and relax on white-sand beaches.
  • Tortuguero National Park: Kayak through canals and see sea turtles nesting.
  • Rincón de la Vieja: Discover waterfalls, volcanic mud pots, and hot springs.
  • Corcovado National Park: Experience one of the most biodiverse places on Earth.
  • Osa Peninsula: Visit secluded beaches and spot whales and dolphins.
  • Poás Volcano: Marvel at the active crater and turquoise sulfuric lake.
  • Tamarindo: Surf world-class waves and enjoy vibrant nightlife.
  • La Fortuna: Explore waterfalls, zip-lines, and local culture."

When is the Best Time to Visit Costa Rica

Costa Rica is a year-round destination, but the best time to visit depends on your preferences:

  • Dry Season (December to April): Perfect for beach vacations and hiking.
  • Green Season (May to November): Ideal for lush landscapes and fewer crowds.
  • Wildlife Viewing: Visit Tortuguero from July to October for turtle nesting or Corcovado for year-round biodiversity.
