Costa Rica Volcanoes

Costa Rica is home to over 200 volcanic formations, with 7 currently active volcanoes. When is the Best Time to Visit Costa Rica

Costa Rica is a year-round destination, but the best time to visit depends on your preferences:

  • Dry Season (December to April): Perfect for beach vacations and hiking.
  • Green Season (May to November): Ideal for lush landscapes and fewer crowds.
  • Wildlife Viewing: Visit Tortuguero from July to October for turtle nesting or Corcovado for year-round biodiversity.

Why Choose Costa Rica for Your Next Adventure?

Biodiversity Like No Other:

Costa Rica is home to 5% of the world’s biodiversity, making it one of the most biologically diverse places on Earth. From scarlet macaws and howler monkeys to sea turtles and jaguars, the country’s wildlife is as diverse as its landscapes. Explore national parks like Corcovado, Manuel Antonio, and Tortuguero to witness this incredible biodiversity up close.

Adventure Awaits:

For thrill-seekers, Costa Rica is a dream destination. Zip-line through the Monteverde Cloud Forest, raft down the thrilling rapids of the Pacuare River, or hike to the summit of Arenal Volcano. With activities like canyoning, surfing, and ATV tours, there’s no shortage of adrenaline-pumping adventures.

Eco-Tourism at Its Best:

Costa Rica is a global leader in sustainable tourism, with over 25% of its land protected as national parks or reserves. Stay in eco-friendly lodges, support local communities, and explore destinations that prioritize conservation. At Nacion Travel, we’re committed to eco-friendly practices that protect Costa Rica’s natural beauty.

Beaches for Every Mood:

With over 800 miles of coastline, Costa Rica offers beaches for every type of traveler. Relax on the white sands of Manuel Antonio, surf the world-class waves of Tamarindo, or explore the secluded shores of the Osa Peninsula. Whether you’re looking for adventure or relaxation, Costa Rica’s beaches have it all.
